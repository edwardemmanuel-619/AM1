What I Offer

01

Sports Massage

Not just for athletes. Ideal for anyone with muscle tension, postural imbalances or an active lifestyle — including desk workers and physically demanding jobs.

  • Breaks down adhesions & scar tissue
  • Increases flexibility & range of motion
  • Flushes out lactic acid
  • Speeds up injury recovery
02

Hot Stone Therapy

Heated volcanic basalt stones penetrate deep into muscles, delivering a level of relaxation that goes beyond a standard massage — ideal for chronic tension and anxiety.

  • Deep muscle penetration via heat
  • Activates the parasympathetic nervous system
  • Improves circulation & oxygen delivery
  • Reduces cortisol for better sleep
03

Head Massage

A targeted treatment for tension headaches, scalp tightness and mental stress — a deeply calming experience that works from the top down.

  • Relieves tension headaches
  • Eases scalp & neck tightness
  • Promotes deep mental relaxation
  • Improves focus & clarity

Meet Your Therapist

Ana Maria Virlan

Hi, I'm Ana Maria — a dedicated Sports Therapist and Personal Trainer with a passion for helping people feel their best: pain-free, stress-free and balanced.

With a background in the fitness industry since 2021, I've worked with athletes and office workers alike. I specialise in Sports Massage, Hot Stone Therapy and Head Massage, focusing on relieving muscle tension, correcting imbalances, and calming the nervous system.
